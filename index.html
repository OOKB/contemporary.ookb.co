The Contemporary, Baltimore, MD.

The Contemporary is temporarily on hiatus as we determine our next steps.

Our plan is to continue on as a robust, imaginative and critically important voice in Baltimore and beyond. Our work now is to evolve as a truly sustainable organization. We remain committed to our mission as a nomadic, non collecting art museum, guided by three main principles: artists matter, collaboration is key, and audience is everywhere.

We wish to thank everyone for their past support and will look to you again for ideas and resources as we move forward. Perhaps Cara Ober, writing for BMore Art, most cogently articulated the value of Contemporary in her article that is here for your reading pleasure.

The Board is taking this time to carefully consider our options in programming, governance and funding. We are considering partnerships and allied opportunities, and we will keep you informed as we progress. We are always interested and open to input as we evolve to our next iteration. We welcome your comments and ideas.

Feel free to contact us, INFO@CONTEMPORARY.ORG

— Members of the Board of the Contemporary
