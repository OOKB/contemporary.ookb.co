The Contemporary

The Board of Trustees of the Contemporary Museum suspended operations in May of 2012 in order to reimagine our mission, vision and operating structure.

We have since regrouped. We are now The Contemporary — shedding the word “Museum” from our title and redefining the capacity in which we are able to present the most innovative art today — and are taking the necessary actions to create a sustainable model that is a viable asset to Baltimore. We are currently conducting a series of thought partner conversations with community leaders and stakeholders to best understand what we can do to add value to the cultural landscape of the Baltimore.

We're excited to be back and will be making a more formal announcement over the coming months. If there are any questions, comments or concerns please feel free to email us at info@contemporary.org
